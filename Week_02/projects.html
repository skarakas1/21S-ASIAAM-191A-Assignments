I am currently working on a project for my Web-mapping and Social Change course with classmates Ray Hsiao and Brian Zamora. We will be working on a community-based mapping project to empower tenants in the LA area. Part of our plan is to utilize an open-source framework to allow user-translation, making the project more inclusive for the many languages spoken in the region.

Here are some of the organizations that inspire this project:

  1. The Los Angeles Tenants Union is a community-based network of tenants helping tenants.
  2. The Anti-Eviction Mapping Project is a non-profit data-visualization project focused on the Bay Area, Los Angeles, and New York City
